BBM was established in the year 2013. BBM is professionally running Organization, serving Healthcare Industry. we "BBM," are one of the trading companies offering complete sales and service support for Medical Equipments and Hospital Management Software. BBM is running by a group of qualified Professional Bio Medical Engineers with more experience in Healthcare Industry. The promoters are with expertise in the field of Medical Industry, Hospital and Business Management. Serious efforts are being made to provide excellence after sales support to its customers. Further company is also keeping its focus on Cardiology, Gynecology and Critical Care Products to develop expertise in these product segments on long term basis. The Company is being directed with vision of addressing the needs of Physicians and providing them high quality, reliable and cost effective Medical Equipments and supplies.
