We manufacture wide range of export quality fabric

SREE ABIRAMI TEX was established in the year 1992, by Mr K.C. Murugesan. And Managing Partners are Mr K.C. Duraisamy and Mr K.C. Thangaraj.

We manufacture wide range of export quality fabric serving domestic and International market through our clients in India.

We produce both white dhotis and colored dhotis with variety of border designs.

These quality products are made by making use of high quality fabric and latest tools & technology at our own premises. We offer these lungies in different sizes, plain & check designs, patterns and vivid colors to cater to the varied requirements of our clients.

Quality and commitment towards client has provided us the advantage and edge over our competitors.

Our vision is to become leading manufacturer of quality fabrics.

Our Mission is to manufacture environmentally sustainable fabrics.

We deal with all type of export quality fabric and produce meeting clients expectation including quality and color shade. We specialize in jacquard , seersucker checker fabric with wide range of yarn.
