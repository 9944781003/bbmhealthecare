Well-Equipped Ambulance Vehicles

Our fleet of 1 ambulances. The tempo traveller ambulances are fitted with a powerful siren and are air-conditioned with luxurious interiors.
The ambulances are equipped with state-of-the-art medical equipment like cardiac monitors, defibrillator, and airway equipment. Oxygen cylinders, life saving drugs, facilities for suctioning is also available. Transport ventilator is available for transport of intubated and ventilated patients.
All the ambulances are equipped with a wireless communication.
System enables efficient communication between the emergency medical technicians and the ambulance control room, located in emergency area regarding patient's status and other information. This helps the Emergency team at the hospital to be fully prepared to receive the patient and make all necessary arrangements, in advance.
